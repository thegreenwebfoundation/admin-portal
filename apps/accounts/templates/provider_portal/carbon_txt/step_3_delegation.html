Configure carbon.txt sharing

Your secondary domains are the domains of your customers, or any other domain which you want to be attributed to you in the Green Web platform.

For providers who resell other services, we recommend doing this by setting up a custom HTTP response header on all the domains you host, advertising the carbon.txt hosted on your primary domain. Configure your web server or proxy to add the CarbonTxt-Location header listed below (see the carbon.txt documentation for further details, and instructions for some popular web servers such as Apache, Nginx and Caddy).
