Carbon.txt is a single recognisable location on any web domain for public sustainability data relating to that company. By combing Carbon.txt with your Green Web Foundation provider listing you can maintain a single source of truth for your organisations sustainability disclosures, and then share them across the different domains your organisation owns. This benefit can also be passed on to your customers, who's domains can also be linked to your hosting provider listing our dataset. This ensures that whenever their domains are checked in our Green Web Check and API the results accurately reflect their use of your services.
