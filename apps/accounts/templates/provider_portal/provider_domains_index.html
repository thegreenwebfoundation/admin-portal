Problems with your Green Web Check results showing the wrong hosting provider name? This solution using carbon.txt is for you. It has three key steps:

  1. Identify a domain you are in complete control of – your primary domain.
  2. Create a carbon.txt file containing your key sustainability disclosures and host it on your primary domain. This makes it your primary linked domain.
  3. Configure that carbon.txt file to be shared across your customers' or other domains.
